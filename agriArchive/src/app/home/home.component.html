About Us

AgriArchive, a website developed with a mission to provide end to end solutions to farmers with regards to the sowing seasons, harvesting season, soil types, fertilizers and manures required, innovative cultivation practices of various crops, etc. which can cater to the needs and requirements of farmers across India. Covid-19 Pandemic had resulted in the shut down large parts of the world economy and it badly affected to the trading, cultivation and feeding of many Indians. This contemplate the need to cultivate agricultural products for our needs, and nowadays, a lots of people have developed an interest in cultivation, but current generation lack enough knowledge about conventional & modern form of agriculture, such as planting, harvesting, fertilizers used for it etc.. Thus, we meant to encourage and promote the new farmers in the industry for adopting various innovative cultivation practices and to use organic farming methods. We also believe that a better and deep understanding of such practices will resort to earn better profits during this Covid-19 pandemic season. We also enable the customers with a platform to shop seeds.
